Key Benefits

Why Switch to a Fan Grid

Redundancy

Running fans in parallel means if one fan goes down, the others keep your system running. No single point of failure.

Up to 50% Energy Savings

Fans can consume up to 50% of HVAC energy costs. Fan grids use EC motors that are dramatically more efficient than legacy axial fans.

Flexibility

Fan grids are configured to fit your building's specific needs. The array can be expanded or downsized as requirements change.

Easy Installation & Maintenance

Swap your old axial fan with a grid quickly, with minimal interruption. Modular design means individual fans can be serviced while others run.
