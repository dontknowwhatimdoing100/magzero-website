Key Benefits

Why Choose MagPak

Plug and Play

Factory-assembled, pre-charged, pre-wired. Minimal field work required. Connect to your chilled water loop and power supply — done.

Fast Deployment

From order to operational in days, not months. Ideal for emergency replacements or fast-track projects with tight deadlines.

Compact Footprint

Engineered to fit in tight mechanical spaces where traditional chillers won't fit. Self-contained design minimizes piping runs.

Energy Efficient

Same TurboCor magnetic bearing technology as MagStack. Oil-free operation delivers 30-50% energy savings over conventional chillers.
