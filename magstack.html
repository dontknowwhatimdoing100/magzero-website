Decentralized Chilled Water

MagStack
Chiller Plant

A compact, indoor, self-contained chilled water system built around Danfoss TurboCor magnetic bearing compressors. Designed to replace or supplement aging central chiller plants.

Key Benefits

Why Choose MagStack

No Central Plant Required

MagStack units distribute throughout your building, eliminating the need for a dedicated mechanical room. Ideal for buildings with limited space.

Magnetic Bearing Compressors

Oil-free, frictionless operation means lower maintenance, longer equipment life, and dramatically higher efficiency vs. traditional compressors.

Modular & Scalable

Start with the capacity you need and add units as load grows. Each MagStack operates independently for true redundancy.

Easy Installation

Pre-engineered and factory-assembled. Fits through standard doorways. Connects to your existing chilled water loop with minimal downtime.
