About
Magzero Technologies

We're an HVAC engineering and equipment company on a mission to make net-zero performance achievable for every commercial building.

Our Story

Magzero Technologies was founded to solve a persistent problem in commercial HVAC: aging chiller plants that waste energy and money.

Our answer is a new generation of equipment built around Danfoss TurboCor magnetic bearing compressors — the most efficient centrifugal compressors on the market. Oil-free, frictionless, and virtually maintenance-free, these compressors are the foundation of everything we build.

Based in Los Angeles, we design, engineer, and support high-efficiency chilled water systems for commercial and industrial buildings across the United States.

What We Do

  • MagStack & MagPak — Decentralized chilled water plants that eliminate the need for traditional central chiller rooms
  • Smart Fan Grids — Modular fan arrays that replace aging cooling tower fans with redundant, energy-efficient alternatives
  • TurboCor Chiller Controller — Purpose-built controls with Autotune optimization for TurboCor compressors
  • Danfoss TurboCor Parts — Genuine OEM spare parts with technical support from engineers who work on these systems daily
