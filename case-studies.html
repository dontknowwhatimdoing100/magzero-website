MagStack Installation

Class A Office Tower — Los Angeles, CA

Challenge

A 350,000 sq ft office building with a 25-year-old central chiller plant consuming excessive energy. The building had no space for a traditional chiller replacement, and the property management team needed a solution that minimized tenant disruption.

Solution

Installed three MagStack decentralized chiller units distributed across mechanical closets on floors 8, 16, and 24. Each unit operates independently with Danfoss TurboCor compressors and Magzero Autotune controls.

Results

42% Energy Reduction
$185K Annual Savings
2.8 yr Payback Period
Zero Tenant Disruption
Fan Grid Retrofit

Regional Medical Center — Phoenix, AZ

Challenge

A 500-bed hospital with two aging cooling towers, each using a single 30HP axial fan. One fan failed during a summer heat wave, leaving the facility critically under-cooled. Redundancy was non-existent.

Solution

Replaced both single-fan assemblies with Magzero Smart Fan Grids — 6 EC motor fans per tower. Installation was completed over two weekends with no interruption to hospital cooling.

Results

38% Energy Reduction
N+1 Redundancy Achieved
12 dB Noise Reduction
2 wknd Installation Time
Controller + Parts

University Campus — San Diego, CA

Challenge

A campus with 4 existing TurboCor-based chillers running on factory default settings. The chillers were functional but not optimized — the BAS integration was minimal and operators had limited visibility into compressor performance.

Solution

Installed a Magzero TurboCor Chiller Controller with Autotune, connecting all 4 compressors. Replaced worn bearing controllers and power modules with genuine Danfoss parts. Full BACnet integration with campus BAS.

Results

28% Additional Savings
$92K Annual Savings
Full BAS Visibility
1.5 yr Payback Period
